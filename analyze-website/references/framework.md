# 14-Dimension Website Analysis Framework

Use this checklist when analyzing a website or product.

## Default report header

```md
# {product-or-site-name} 网站分析

分析日期：{YYYY-MM-DD}
分析对象：{name + URL}
分析方法：基于 14 维网站/产品分析框架。
说明：官网事实、第三方公开信息、以及推断应明确区分。
```

## Dimensions

### 1. 这个产品解决的是什么问题？

Cover:

- core problem
- workflow being replaced
- why this is painful or repetitive
- whether the product is a single-point tool or a broader platform

### 2. 用户是谁？

Cover:

- primary user
- buyer vs end user
- company size or role segmentation
- industry segmentation if visible

### 3. 用户为什么需要它？

Cover:

- urgency and frequency
- time, cost, quality, or compliance benefits
- why existing alternatives are insufficient

### 4. 用户是如何评价它的？

Cover:

- official testimonials
- third-party ratings and review counts
- recurring praise themes
- recurring complaint themes

### 5. 它是如何找到用户的？

Cover:

- SEO
- direct traffic / brand search
- content marketing
- community
- paid channels
- integrations or platform distribution
- enterprise sales

### 6. 它赚钱吗？多少？

Cover:

- pricing model
- packaging
- enterprise tier if any
- what is known vs what is estimated
- estimated range, not false precision

### 7. 我从这个产品身上学到了什么我以前不知道的东西？

Cover:

- product insight
- market insight
- GTM insight
- workflow or packaging insight

### 8. 它的什么做法，我并不容易？为什么？

Cover:

- model quality
- data advantage
- workflow integration depth
- infrastructure scale
- brand or distribution moat
- enterprise capability

### 9. 如果我是产品的作者，我如何只用一句话，把产品推销给用户？

Write a one-line pitch in the style of the founder or homepage.

### 10. 如果我做一个产品解决同样场景的同样问题，我可能采用哪些不同方法？

Prefer differentiated strategies, not superficial feature swaps.

Examples:

- verticalize by industry
- move down-market or up-market
- change pricing mechanics
- focus on workflow instead of point solution
- prioritize privacy, collaboration, automation, or compliance

### 11. 我能做出来吗？需要什么资源？

Split into:

- MVP feasibility
- scale-stage difficulty
- people needed
- infra/data/tooling needed
- realistic constraint assessment

### 12. 我如何找到用户？

Turn the target's observed GTM into an actionable acquisition plan:

- search landing pages
- templates
- plugins
- partnerships
- affiliates
- community
- outbound
- developer distribution

### 13. 为什么是我？我有什么独特之处？

Frame as founder-product fit:

- domain understanding
- technical edge
- distribution edge
- access to customer segment
- credibility with the buyer

### 14. 我喜欢这个产品吗？

Make this reflective, but grounded in:

- quality of problem
- attractiveness of economics
- defensibility
- difficulty level
- whether you would personally want to build in this space

## Default ending

```md
## 总结

{2-4 paragraph synthesis of positioning, GTM, monetization, and moat}

## 参考来源

### 官方来源
- ...

### 第三方来源
- ...
```
